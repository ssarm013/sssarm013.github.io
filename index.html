I love working with children and have experience dealing with children and young adults who have learning and developmental disabilities. The ages vary from 1-3 years old, and the oldest I have helped was around 16 years old. I am patient, understanding and extremely sympathetic when I am working with my students.

I love to help my students improve on their learning and writing skills.Specifically, students who have a difficult time with comprehending common core information within literature. I also strive to lend a helping hand when they struggle to stay academically focused with homework, tests, quizzes, and etc. In order to help students improve, I evaluate their reading and writing skills by producing quizzes. After that, I simply group the students by what they scored from the quizzes. Then, I separate the work that needs to be done for the students. So, when it comes to completing work, each student completes a worksheet that was designed for their own abilities. Therefore, challenging each student effectively by having them work on what skill they need to improve on.

I have written books, poems, and short stories for my students so they can learn how literature comes in different forms. This allows them to explore more with language and helps their minds develop more when it comes to real world situations. Not only that, but I have also created stop motion clips, and comic books, so they can see impactful stories through vivid imagery.
